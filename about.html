About Me

I use my creative and compositional skills to generate strong images within the scope of food photography.

I was born and raised in Bogota - Colombia, I studied Photography at the Taller Cinco University Corporation, where I also obtained a diploma in Art Direction. I am currently based in Atlanta, Georgia in the United States.

My work is focused for all those who need to make use of the images as an advertising, editorial, decorative and other resources, always aiming to transmit the sensations that food can generate in people.

We generate fears while we sit. We overcome them by action.
